Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11/01/2022

Your privacy is important to us, and protecting your data is a responsibility that we embrace. Please read this privacy policy to understand what we collect and why, and how we are dedicated to protecting your information.

WHAT WE COLLECT​

VISITORS​

We do not collect any personally identifiable information about visitors to our website, unless provided voluntarily through account creation or submitting a contact request.

CUSTOMER INFORMATION​

We may use your name and email address to send you transactional emails, account change notifications, software upgrades and information about new products, which you can opt out of at any time.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES​

We will never sell, share, or rent your information to any partners or third party service providers. You can update or remove what you have shared with us at our website, or you can request account deletion by email at support@iasql.com

We use Posthog on the https://iasql.com domains to collect anonymous user data through our website for the purposes of understanding our audience better, as well as conversion tracking, including the storing and accessing of cookies, and you agree to allow us to carry out these activities. These third-party services may use this data to serve ads targeted to users’ interests.

LAW & ORDER​

We may disclose your information to third parties if we determine that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

(a) comply with the law; (b) protect any person from death or serious bodily injury; (c) prevent fraud or abuse of the software or our users; or (d) protect The Company’s property rights. We will abide by the following principles when receiving and responding to government requests for our users' data:

(a) transparency, (b) protect all users, and (c) provide trusted services.

SECURITY​

We are dedicated to keeping your information secure and are proactive at testing for vulnerabilities. We utilize industry-standard practices to protect your information from loss or misuse, such as secure protocols and encryption to protect the transmission of personal information.

ACQUISITION​

If we are involved in a reorganization, merger, acquisition or sale of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that deal. We will notify you at the email address associated with your account of any such deal and outline your options in that event.

POLICY CHANGES​

We may revise this Privacy Policy when necessary, and will post the most current version on our website. If a revision meaningfully reduces your rights, we will notify you by the email you have provided to us.

QUESTIONS​

Have questions or concerns about our company, our services and privacy? Contact us at support@iasql.com
